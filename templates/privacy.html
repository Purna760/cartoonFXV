Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CartoonFX. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our video-to-cartoon conversion service.

2. Information We Collect

Video Files: When you upload a video for processing, we temporarily store it on our servers solely for the purpose of converting it to a cartoon-style video. These files are automatically deleted after processing and download.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, such as the number of videos processed and general usage patterns. This data does not identify individual users.

Contact Information: If you contact us through our contact form, we collect the information you provide, such as your name and email address, solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and transform your video files
  • Provide customer support when you contact us
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our platform

4. Data Retention

Your uploaded videos and processed files are automatically deleted from our servers after you download the processed video or within 24 hours, whichever comes first. We do not retain copies of your videos for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transfers are encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not share your uploaded videos or personal information with any third parties. Our video processing is performed entirely on our own servers using OpenCV technology.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about how your data is processed
  • Request deletion of any personal data we hold about you
  • Opt out of any communications from us

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies that are necessary for the operation of our website. These cookies do not track you across other websites and are used solely to maintain your session while using CartoonFX.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
